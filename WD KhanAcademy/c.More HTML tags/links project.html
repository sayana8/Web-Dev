Recipe #1

Perfect pancakes are easier to make than you think. This pancake recipe produces thick, fluffy, and all-around delicious pancakes with just a few ingredients that are probably already in your kitchen (and it's so much better than the boxed stuff).

  • Time: 20 mins
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Flour: 200 g
Baking powder: 15 g
Sugar: 80g
Milk 150 g
butter 20g

Step 1: Sift the dry ingredients together.

Step 2: Make a well, then add the wet ingredients. Stir to combine.

Step 3: Scoop the batter onto a hot griddle or pan.

Step 4: Cook for two to three minutes, then flip.

Step 5: Continue cooking until brown on both sides.
